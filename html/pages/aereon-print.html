Project Details

Aereon Inc needed printed marketing collateral for their newly branded company and product lines. I worked on materials that were 8.5x11 double sided 4 color press prints, 8.5x11 tri-fold pamplets, and 8.5x11 6,8, and 12 page brochures. I worked on these designs, pre-press, print proofs, and final production.
