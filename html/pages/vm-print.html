Project Details

A local musician in Louisville, KY was getting ready for her first album release party. She wanted some shirts for the release. She wanted to include some art work of her friend, and then asked me to make a shirt design that would incorporate her artwork and my design idea. We came together to make this phases of the moon design with a monochromatic blue overlay.

  • Client: Violet Moon
  • Date: 01 September, 2017
